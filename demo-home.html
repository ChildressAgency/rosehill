Northern Virginia's Premier Bird Hunting Preserve.

Historic Rose Hill Farm has been owned by the Ashby/Covington family since the 1800's. Having always enjoyed hunting and the natural outdoors we now offer our 210 acres of prime habitat where upland preserve bird shooting is as natural as you will find anywhere.

Gallery >>

Pheasants, Chukar, Quail OH MY!

Come experience the thrill of the hunt with a guided or un-guided trip. We offer rental equipment as well as trained dogs, but we invite you to bring your own trained retriever to the grounds for an un-guided tour. Choose what activity you would like and call today to reserve your spot!

Upland Bird Hunt

Pheasant, chukar, quail, or a mix of birds will be placed in the ground cover a field in preperation for your guided or non-guided upland bird hunt.
