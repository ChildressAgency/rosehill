Have a question? So have others before you, read below for some of the most commonly asked questions.

Do you rent guns?

Yes, we rent shotguns for $20. We have a limited availability so please let us know head of time if you require one.

Do you sell ammunition?

Yes, we sell 12, 20 and a very limited amount of 16 gauge shells.

What type of ammunition do you recommend?

Depending on what you choose to hunt we recommend using 7 ½ shot for 12 gauge - no shot larger than 6, and for 20 gauge we recommend 6 shot.

Do I need a hunting license?

Yes, in order to hunt at Rose Hill Game Preserve a license is required. All Virginia residents are required to have the Virginia Resident Hunting License. All non-residents are required to have the Non Resident Shooting Preserve License. These licenses can be purchased from the Virginia Department of Game and Inland Fisheries website (http://www.dgif.virginia.gov/licenses/) Each license is $23 and is valid for 1 year. You're license must be with you during your hunt, so please be sure to print out a copy prior to arriving. Customers can also purchase a 3 day license or a preserve license at a lesser cost from Virginia Game & IInland Fisheries.
